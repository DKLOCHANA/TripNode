Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Section 01

Introduction

Welcome to TripNode. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered travel planning application.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Section 02

Information We Collect

a. Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (securely encrypted)

b. Trip & Travel Data

To generate personalised itineraries, we collect:

  • Destination preferences and searches
  • Travel dates and duration
  • Interests (e.g., Culture, Foodie, Adventure, Relax)
  • Budget information
  • AI-generated itineraries you save

c. Subscription and Payment Information

Subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store or access your payment details — payment data is handled securely by the respective app stores.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate personalised AI travel itineraries based on your preferences
  • Save and display your trips in "My Trips"
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscriptions and manage your PRO membership
  • Improve app performance and AI recommendation quality
  • Send notifications relevant to your trips (if enabled)

Section 04

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of account credentials
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Industry-standard HTTPS communication

No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We do, however, implement industry-standard practices to safeguard your data.

Section 05

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, AI services, analytics, subscription management)
  • If required by applicable law
  • To protect the rights or safety of users or third parties

Section 06

Account Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time by:

  • Using the in-app "Delete Account" option in Profile & Settings, or
  • Contacting us at support@tripnode.app

When your account is deleted, your personal data and saved itineraries will be permanently removed from our servers.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.

Section 09

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: support@tripnode.app
